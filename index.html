The Five Events
How It Works
  • Round Robin: 5 rounds — each round runs 3 different games at once (all stations busy), and every team plays 4 different games.
  • Seeding: by wins → head-to-head → dart-off tiebreaker.
  • Championship: all 7 teams; #1 seed byes to the semis; quarters & semis are single games; final is best 2 of 3.
  • Higher seed picks the game — but not the same game the first two rounds.
  • NO FOUL PLAY. EVERYONE MUST HAVE FUN. 🇺🇸
